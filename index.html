About Magical Innovation Hub.

Magical innovation hub aims to entertain and educate people regardless of their age, gender, nationality and language. We established in 2016 in Trivandrum district of Kerala state.

We provide multiple solutions for magicians in the form of magic-master classes, marketing workshops, magic equipment making and selling, and magic equipment rental services at magic show collaborations.

We entertain the public with different forms of Magic like mentalism, stage-illusions Conjuring acts and close-up magic. Our shows are fit for mega-events, birthday parties, kids' parties, corporate events, awareness programs and any happy occasions.

Mission & Vision

#Produce practice perform

Magical innovation hub is the dream of an Indian magician to bring the standards of Indian magic performance to an international level.


We look forward to creating opportunities to magicians to enhance their performance level to the best.
